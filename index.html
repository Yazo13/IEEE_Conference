About the Conference

The Annual Climate Conference 2024 brings together experts, professionals, and enthusiasts from around the world to explore innovative solutions in "Climate Solutions for a Sustainable Future." Join us in ISEC from June 20-22, 2024, as we address current challenges, share knowledge, and collaborate for a better future.

Dates: June 20-22, 2024
